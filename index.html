Honda CBR 300R

Ahh yes, the CBR300R – a lightweight, easy to maneuver, easy to maintain, fuel-sipping, beginner bike prodigy. That may sound like a lofty description, but the CBR300R has been that since its introduction in 2013 (on sale since in 2015). The CBR300R has successfully cemented itself in the hearts and minds of the entry-level faithful.

Purchase Information

The CBR300R is powered by the 286cc, liquid cooled, mono cylinder engine that kicks out a maximum power of 30.4 bhp at 8500 rpm and a maximum torque of 27 Nm at 7250 rpm.
